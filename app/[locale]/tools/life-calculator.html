Frequently asked questions

How many days have I been alive?

Enter your date of birth above and our calculator instantly shows you the exact number of days, weeks, hours, and even heartbeats you've accumulated. The average human lives about 29,200 days — how many have you used?

How many heartbeats does a person have in a lifetime?

The average resting heart beats 70 times per minute, which means about 2.5 billion heartbeats in a 70-year lifetime. Enter your age to see your personal count.

How much money will I have if I save every month?

Use our retirement planner slider above. Even saving ₹2,000 per month at 8% annual return for 30 years grows to over ₹30 lakhs. Starting early is the single most powerful financial move.

What is the life expectancy in India?

The average life expectancy in India is approximately 69–70 years as of recent data. Select your country in the calculator for a personalized life progress bar based on your region's average.

How much does smoking cost over a lifetime?

At just ₹100 per day, smoking costs ₹36,500 per year — and if that same money were invested at 10% annual return, it would be worth over ₹6 lakhs in 10 years. Use the habit calculator above to see your real number.
